Honest verdict
The backend cognitive system is production-grade and genuinely complete — algorithms, memory, automation, planning, security, scheduling, all wired. What's missing is the surface layer: a frontend, 3 security lines, and connector adapters. The app cannot ship to a user today because there is no screen to look at. That is the only remaining blocker for an MVP.
